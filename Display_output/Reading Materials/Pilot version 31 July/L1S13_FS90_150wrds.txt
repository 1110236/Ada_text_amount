The Earth is a planet. It is part of a group of planets. They move around a star. It is called the Sun. This is all called the "Solar System." The Earth is the third planet from the Sun. It is made of rock. The planets next to it are also made of rock. They are Venus and Mars. The outer planets are made of gas. Some of the rock is hard. The land we live on is hard. Some of it is liquid. Under the land it is very hot. The rocks melt. This is called "magma." The land floats on it. At the center of the Earth is the "core." It is in two parts. Both are made of metals. They are nickel and iron. The outer layer is a bit soft. The inner layer is solid.
